# Bark

> This guide applies to firmware v4.9 and later. For earlier versions, please click [here](parental_control.md#bark-version).

The [Bark](https://www.bark.us/){target="_blank"} service can help protect your child's digital world and provide comprehensive online protection. It typically requires a paid subscription. However, as part of our partnership with Bark, GL.iNet offers the Bark Home plan for free **on select router models**, providing advanced monitoring and alerts at no extra cost.

**Note**: 

1. The Bark service is available **only in the United States, Australia, and South Africa**. Click [here](https://support.bark.us/hc/en-us/articles/360049965072-International-availability){target="_blank"} for details.

2. Bark and GL.iNet Parental Control cannot be enabled simultaneously, as this may trigger conflicts.

## Quick Setup

Log in to the router's web admin panel and navigate to **APPLICATIONS** -> **Bark**.

![bark](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark1.png){class="glboxshadow"}

Toggle the switch to enable Bark, then click **Apply**. 

![bark enable](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark2.png){class="glboxshadow"}

Next, pair your device with your Bark account. Click **Device Pairing Link** to pair this router with your Bark account.

![bark pairing link](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark3.png){class="glboxshadow"}

You will be redirected to the Bark website. Follow the on-screen instructions to log in, pair your device, create a profile and finish the initial setup.

![bark login](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark4.png){class="glboxshadow"}
<small>(Bark login)</small>

![bark paired](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark5.png){class="glboxshadow"}
<small>(Device paired)</small>

![bark profile](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark6.png){class="glboxshadow"}
<small>(Initial setup)</small>

![bark setup](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark7.png){class="glboxshadow"}
<small>(Create a profile)</small>

Once your device connects to Bark Cloud services and is paired with your account, the router's web Admin Panel will be displayed as follows.

![bark status](https://static.gl-inet.com/docs/router/en/4/interface_guide/bark/bark8.png){class="glboxshadow"}

Click **Go to Bark** or [here](https://www.bark.us/app/children/?ref=glinet&home=glinet) to log in to the Bark dashboard and set up your parental control rules.

Since GL.iNet is not the provider of this service, should you encounter any issues while using Bark, kindly reach out directly to [Bark's Technical Support](https://www.bark.us/contact-us/?ref=glinet&home=glinet) for assistance.
